Healthy Eating

A healthy eating plan gives your body the nutrients it needs every day while staying within your
daily calorie goal for weight loss. A healthy eating plan also will lower your risk for heart disease
and other health conditions.Everyone has a personal calorie limit. Staying within yours can help
you get to or maintain a healthy weight. Reaching a healthier weight is a balancing act. The
secret is learning how to balance your "energy in" and "energy out" over the long run.
"Energy in" is the calories from foods and beverages you have each day. "Energy out" is the calories you burn for basic body functions and physical activity.
